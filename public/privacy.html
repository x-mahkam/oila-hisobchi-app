MAXFIYLIK SIYOSATI

Oxirgi yangilanish: 9-iyul, 2026-yil

"Oila Hisobchi" ilovasi foydalanuvchilarning shaxsiy ma'lumotlarini himoya qilishni o'zining asosiy tamoyillaridan biri deb hisoblaydi. Ushbu Maxfiylik siyosati ilovadan foydalanganda qanday ma'lumotlar yig'ilishi, qanday maqsadlarda ishlatilishi, qanday saqlanishi va foydalanuvchining huquqlari haqida ma'lumot beradi.

1. Yig'iladigan ma'lumotlar

Ilovaning to'liq ishlashi uchun quyidagi ma'lumotlar yig'ilishi mumkin:

Hisob ma'lumotlari

  • Ism va familiya
  • Elektron pochta manzili
  • Telefon raqami (agar foydalanuvchi kiritsa)
  • Profil rasmi (ixtiyoriy)

Moliyaviy ma'lumotlar

Foydalanuvchi tomonidan ixtiyoriy ravishda kiritilgan:

  • Daromadlar
  • Xarajatlar
  • Qarzlar
  • Jamg'armalar
  • Maqsadlar
  • Oilaviy byudjet ma'lumotlari

Bolalar akkaunti

Agar ota-ona bola akkauntini yaratsa, quyidagi ma'lumotlar saqlanishi mumkin:

  • Ismi
  • Tug'ilgan yili
  • Jinsi (ixtiyoriy)
  • Bajarilgan topshiriqlar
  • Olingan mukofotlar
  • Coin va balans
  • Orzular ro'yxati
  • Bilim o'yinlari natijalari

Bolaga oid barcha ma'lumotlar faqat ota-ona tomonidan yaratiladi va boshqariladi. Ilova bolalardan mustaqil ravishda shaxsiy ma'lumot yig'maydi.

2. Ma'lumotlardan foydalanish

Ma'lumotlar quyidagi maqsadlarda ishlatiladi:

  • Oilaviy hisoblarni yuritish;
  • Daromad va xarajatlarni boshqarish;
  • Oila a'zolari o'rtasida ma'lumotlarni sinxronlashtirish;
  • Bolalar topshiriqlarini boshqarish;
  • Bilim o'yinlari va mukofot tizimini ishlatish;
  • AI tavsiyalarini shakllantirish;
  • Xavfsizlikni ta'minlash;
  • Texnik nosozliklarni aniqlash va bartaraf etish;
  • Ilovani takomillashtirish.

3. Bolalar maxfiyligi

"Oila Hisobchi" Google Play Families Policy talablariga amal qiladi.

  • Bola akkaunti faqat ota-ona tomonidan yaratiladi.
  • Bola haqidagi barcha ma'lumotlar ota-ona nazorati ostida saqlanadi.
  • Biz bolalardan mustaqil ravishda shaxsiy ma'lumot yig'maymiz.
  • Ota-ona istalgan vaqtda bola akkauntini o'zgartirishi yoki butunlay o'chirishi mumkin.

4. Ma'lumotlarni saqlash

Ma'lumotlar Google Firebase xizmatlaridan foydalanilgan holda xavfsiz serverlarda saqlanadi.

Xizmatlar:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (agar bildirishnomalar yoqilgan bo'lsa)

Ma'lumotlarni uzatishda zamonaviy xavfsizlik protokollari (TLS) qo'llaniladi.

5. Ma'lumotlarni uchinchi shaxslarga berish

Biz foydalanuvchilarning shaxsiy ma'lumotlarini sotmaymiz va tijorat maqsadlarida uchinchi shaxslarga bermaymiz.

Ma'lumotlar faqat quyidagi hollarda uzatilishi mumkin:

  • qonunchilik talab qilganda;
  • foydalanuvchi roziligi bilan;
  • Google Firebase infratuzilmasi orqali xizmat ko'rsatish uchun.

6. Reklama va tahlil

Ilova hozirda foydalanuvchilarga yo'naltirilgan reklama ko'rsatmaydi.

Agar kelajakda reklama yoki analitika xizmatlari qo'shilsa, ushbu Maxfiylik siyosati yangilanadi.

7. Premium xizmatlar

Premium obunalar Google Play Billing tizimi orqali amalga oshiriladi.

To'lov ma'lumotlari Google tomonidan qayta ishlanadi.

"Oila Hisobchi" foydalanuvchilarning bank kartasi ma'lumotlarini saqlamaydi va ularga kira olmaydi.

8. Foydalanuvchi huquqlari

Foydalanuvchi quyidagi huquqlarga ega:

  • ma'lumotlarini ko'rish;
  • ma'lumotlarini tahrirlash;
  • akkauntini o'chirish;
  • bola akkauntlarini boshqarish;
  • ilovadan foydalanishni istalgan vaqtda to'xtatish.

Akkaunt o'chirilgandan so'ng, foydalanuvchining shaxsiy ma'lumotlari amaldagi qonunchilik va texnik imkoniyatlarga muvofiq o'chiriladi.

9. Maxfiylik siyosatiga o'zgartirishlar

Ushbu Maxfiylik siyosati vaqti-vaqti bilan yangilanishi mumkin.

Muhim o'zgarishlar ilova ichida yoki rasmiy sahifalar orqali foydalanuvchilarga ma'lum qilinadi.

10. Bog'lanish

Agar sizda ushbu Maxfiylik siyosati bo'yicha savollar bo'lsa, biz bilan bog'lanishingiz mumkin.

Финансовые данные

Детский аккаунт

  • Управление заданиями детей;
  • Работа обучающих игр и системы наград;
  • Формирование AI-рекомендаций;
  • Обеспечение безопасности;
  • Выявление и устранение технических неполадок;
  • Улучшение приложения.

3. Конфиденциальность детей

«Oila Hisobchi» соблюдает требования Google Play Families Policy.

  • Детский аккаунт создаётся только родителем.
  • Все данные о ребёнке хранятся под контролем родителя.

  • Родитель может в любое время изменить или полностью удалить детский аккаунт.

Данные хранятся на защищённых серверах с использованием сервисов Google Firebase.

Сервисы:

Мы не продаём персональные данные пользователей и не передаём их третьим лицам в коммерческих целях.

For the app to function fully, the following data may be collected:

Account information

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if provided by the user)
  • Profile picture (optional)

Financial information

Voluntarily entered by the user:

  • Income
  • Expenses
  • Debts
  • Savings
  • Goals
  • Family budget data

Child accounts

If a parent creates a child account, the following data may be stored:

  • Name
  • Year of birth
  • Gender (optional)
  • Completed tasks
  • Earned rewards
  • Coins and balance
  • Wish list

All data related to a child is created and managed only by the parent. The app does not collect personal data from children independently.

2. How We Use Data

Data is used for the following purposes:

  • Keeping family financial records;
  • Managing income and expenses;
  • Synchronizing data between family members;
  • Managing children's tasks;
  • Running learning games and the reward system;
  • Generating AI recommendations;
  • Ensuring security;
  • Detecting and fixing technical issues;
  • Improving the app.

3. Children's Privacy

"Oila Hisobchi" complies with the Google Play Families Policy.

  • A child account can only be created by a parent.
  • All data about a child is stored under parental control.
  • We do not collect personal data from children independently.
  • A parent may modify or completely delete a child account at any time.

4. Data Storage

Data is stored on secure servers using Google Firebase services.

Services:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (if notifications are enabled)

Modern security protocols (TLS) are used when transmitting data.

5. Sharing Data with Third Parties

We do not sell users' personal data and do not share it with third parties for commercial purposes.

  • when required by law;

6. Advertising and Analytics

The app currently does not show targeted advertising to users.

If advertising or analytics services are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated.

7. Premium Services

Premium subscriptions are processed through the Google Play Billing system.

Payment information is processed by Google.

"Oila Hisobchi" does not store users' bank card details and has no access to them.

8. User Rights

Users have the following rights:

  • to view their data;
  • to edit their data;
  • to delete their account;
  • to manage child accounts;
  • to stop using the app at any time.

After an account is deleted, the user's personal data is deleted in accordance with applicable law and technical capabilities.
